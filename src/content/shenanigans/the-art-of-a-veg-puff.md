The veg puff is not merely a snack. It is a small architectural event wrapped in paper.

## The crust as structure

A proper veg puff has a roof, walls, and a controlled failure system. The flakes are not accidents. They are proof that the structure is under delicious stress.

## The filling distribution problem

The finest puff does not reveal all its potato at once. It creates suspense. The first bite is pastry, the second bite is negotiation, the third bite is the reveal.

## The temperature window

Too hot and the filling becomes a legal issue. Too cold and the puff turns into a file folder. The correct temperature is warm enough to inspire trust and dangerous enough to command respect.

## The paper bag matters

The paper bag is part of the ritual. It absorbs oil, preserves mystery, and gives you something to hold while pretending this is not your second puff.

## Conclusion

The veg puff is a humble masterpiece: flaky, chaotic, slightly oily, and impossible to eat without leaving evidence.
